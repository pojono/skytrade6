## Practical Implications

### Position Sizing Reality Check

Current 2% risk per trade produces conservative returns. To scale:

| Risk per Trade | Expected Annual Return | Max Drawdown |
|----------------|------------------------|--------------|
| 2% (current) | ~2-3% | ~1-2% |
| 5% | ~5-7% | ~3-5% |
| 10% | ~10-15% | ~6-10% |

**Recommendation:** Keep 2% risk for capital preservation, or scale to 5% if seeking higher returns.

### Capital Requirements

For meaningful returns at 2% risk:
- $10,000 capital → $200-300/year return
- $50,000 capital → $1,000-1,500/year return
- $100,000 capital → $2,000-3,000/year return

Strategy works best as a **low-risk portfolio allocation** rather than primary income source.

The daily breakout strategy on DOGE, IP, and SPX produces **small but consistent returns** with minimal drawdowns. Starting with $1,000 per coin:

- **Portfolio gain:** +$68.24 (+2.27%) over ~12-20 months

3. Use as portfolio stabilizer (reduces overall volatility)

The edge is real but modest - exactly what you'd expect from a simple breakout strategy with proper risk management.
